The Workshop

BUILT ON
GREASE &
GRIT

Aarti Bikers was born from a single bay over two decades ago. Today, our facility blends the soul of an old-school mechanical garage with the precision of a modern workshop — exposed brick walls, worn concrete floors, and the smell of engine oil mixed with the glow of LED workstation lighting. With 25+ years of garage services and 11+ years dedicated to bike excellence, we're the trusted name riders swear by.

  • 6 Modern Service Bays
  • Glass-Enclosed Inspection Office
  • Vintage Tool Rack Collection
  • Digital Diagnostic Centre
